Cyan Dream Creations

Color Palette

A dark celestial palette built around glowing cyan, dreamy purples, and warm gold accents. The system is designed to feel mystical, premium, and artistic while still staying readable for Etsy banners, product mockups, brand graphics, and future web design.

Core Brand Colors

These are the signature tones that define Cyan Dream Creations. Use the dark night shades for structure, the cyan family for recognition, the purples for magical atmosphere, and the gold sparingly for luxury and sparkle.

Cyan Glow

#2FE6FF
Primary logo glow Hero accent

Your signature electric cyan. This is the most recognizable brand color and should lead the visual identity.

Cyan Tide

#1FBAD6
Primary brand color Buttons

Deep Teal

Lavender Glow

Recommended Use Cases

Logo System

Use Cyan Glow for the primary script, Stardust Gold for stars and trim, and Midnight Base or Moonlit Navy behind it for contrast.

Etsy Banner

Keep the background predominantly Midnight Base and Night Teal, then layer Cyan Glow and small amounts of gold for celestial focal points.

Product Mockups

Use darker surfaces to let bright cyan art stand out. Bring in Dream Indigo and Lavender Glow for magical accents and gemstone details.

Website or Shop Graphics

Use Moonlit Navy panels, Icy Mist text, Cyan Tide buttons, and soft gold dividers for a polished premium feel.

Suggested brand balance: 80% dark night tones, 15% cyan family, 5% gold and purple accents. This keeps the brand rich and cohesive instead of busy.
